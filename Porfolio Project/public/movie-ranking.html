The Best Movies According to Tanya

My Top 3 Movies of all Time

Star Wars

Cult Classic! You will learn a lot about modern society based on this one. The best part is its analogue of the WWII

Star Trek

Another Cult Classic! Built maybe as an attempted sequel of what intergalictic society would look like post Star Wars.
The Federation is the hope for what our Society could look like.

Lord of the Rings

A wonderful story of adventure, brotherhood and fighting for something bigger than yourself.
It inspires us to do more.
